Our Story

Built On Bold, Cooked With Care

Urban Spice opened in 2018 with a simple idea: the city's dining rooms deserved the same boldness as its night markets. Our kitchen draws on spice traditions from Marrakech to Mumbai to Bangkok, reworked through modern technique and produce from local growers.

Every plate starts with a single spice, never a shortcut. We grind, toast and blend in-house daily, so the heat you taste was built that morning, not poured from a jar.
